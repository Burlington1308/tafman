Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my Shower Pressure so Weak?

A weak shower pressure may be due to the accumulation of debris and other waste substance that has clogged your shower head.

Can I fix a blocked toilet myself?

We strongly advise against it. A blocked toilet is problematic for your home and can quickly turn into a health risk if waste is not disposed of properly. Call us immediately to avoid a problematic situation.

Why is my tap leaking?

Rubber washers on faucets wear out over time from valve seat friction and cause leaks. To stop the leak, you need to replace the washers.
